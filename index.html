Our Story

It all started with Grandpa Glenn's riding mower...

Back in the summer of '23, three high school friends found themselves looking for honest work in Hebron. Spencer's grandpa Glenn—known by everyone in town as the guy with the greenest lawn on Maple Street—needed help keeping up with his growing landscaping business.

That's how Larsen, Spencer, and Josh ended up spending their summer sweating under the Ohio sun, learning the difference between a weed and a wildflower, and discovering that perfect lawn stripes require both science and art.

"A lawn isn't just grass—it's the first handshake your home gives to the world. Make it firm, make it warm, and make it green."

— Grandpa Glenn's Golden Rule

By August, we realized we'd caught the landscaping bug. More importantly, we'd caught Grandpa Glenn's passion for transforming outdoor spaces. When he retired that fall, he didn't just pass us his equipment—he passed us his reputation.
